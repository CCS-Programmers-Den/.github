# CCS Programmers' Den

A student organization from the **College of Computer Studies, Tarlac State University** that provides an environment for sharing knowledge, building skills, and growing together as programmers.

## Who We Are

A community of CCS students passionate about programming, technology, and collaborative learning. We aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world software development.

## What We Do

- Host programming workshops, events, and competitions
- Share resources and knowledge across CCS students
- Build projects that sharpen technical skills
- Connect students with opportunities in tech
